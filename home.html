MY Projects

Sustainable Development Goals

In this project, I designed an app, ROJGAAR that helps job seekers find sustainable and ethical job opportunities. The app allows users to create a profile, search for job openings, and apply for positions that align with their values and interests. this project demonstrates my skills in user experience design, visual design, and prototyping, and showcases my commitment to creating solutions that promote sustainability and ethical practices in the workforce.

Students & Professionals-Work From Home

In this project, I designed a connected FOOTREST and developed an accompanying app to enhance the comfort and productivity of users who work from home. Through user research and iterative design, I created a high-fidelity prototype that meets the needs and preferences of my target audience. I believe this connected footrest has the potential to improve the lives of many people who work from home by promoting comfort and productivity.
